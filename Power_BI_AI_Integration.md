## FEATURES 01 & 02: CORE ECOSYSTEM 

**Smart Follow-Up Brief + Smart Activity Log** 

* **The Challenge:** Preparing for a single customer call requires opening several dashboards, reading through activity logs, checking purchase history, verifying item availability, and reviewing pricing - all manually before the call even begins.
* **The Solution:** Two features that form a closed loop: the Activity Log captures what happened (sales writes freely, AI extracts the meaning), and the Follow-Up Brief surfaces everything relevant before the next contact.
* **Free-Form Log + AI Extraction:** Sales writes naturally after each call no forms or structured fields required. The AI reads the entry and automatically extracts: which products were mentioned, what the customer's intent is, timeline, any pricing signals, and the recommended next action. Activity notes are already being logged the next step is having the system extract key actions and surface them automatically before each call.
* **Pre-Call Brief:** Before every customer contact, sales receives a one-page summary: last conversation highlights, products the customer has asked about or purchased, their customer category, and context-aware product suggestions relevant to their profile. Rich purchase history already exists per account this is the data the AI will use to build each pre-call brief.
* **Context-Aware Recommendations:** Suggestions are tailored to the customer's segment. For example, a school-category account will see product suggestions popular among other school accounts not a generic list.

## FEATURE 03: MANAGEMENT 

**Portfolio Coverage View** 

* **The Challenge:** With a large portfolio of customers, it's hard to know at a glance who hasn't been contacted in a while. Accounts that need attention often only surface after a problem has already developed whether that's a complaint, a missed order, or a customer quietly moving to a competitor.
* **The Solution:** A single-view grid showing every customer and lead - with when they were last contacted, their current health score, their stage, and how long they've been inactive. Sales can proactively plan their coverage each week, rather than reacting to gaps after the fact.
* **Portfolio Grid:** All customers and leads displayed in one view: name, category, last contact date, activity gap, stage, and health score sortable and filterable in real time.
* **Smart Filters & Sorting:** Sales can quickly surface the right accounts by filtering on customer category, gap duration, account value, or health score to find today's priorities in seconds.
* **Dormant Account Recovery:** Accounts that have been quiet for an extended period are surfaced and ranked by reactivation potential - helping sales invest outreach effort where it's most likely to pay off. A significant number of active accounts carry strong all-time revenue a coverage view makes it easy to plan proactive outreach before gaps widen. Contact activity decreased year-over-year - a portfolio view helps ensure every account stays within a consistent outreach cadence.

## FEATURE 05: OPERATIONS 

**Daily Sales Command Center**

## FEATURE 06: INTELLIGENCE 

**Buying Season Radar** 

The seasonal buying patterns for each customer segment are already in your data - but without a proactive alert system, these windows of opportunity are often missed or only recognized after the peak has passed. Seasonal patterns are already present in the data proactive alerts ensure the team is positioned ahead of each buying window.

* **Proactive Season Alerts:** Two to four weeks before a customer segment typically enters its buying peak, sales receive an alert listing exactly which accounts and leads in that segment should be contacted now.
* **Category Heatmap:** A visual calendar showing peak buying months for each customer group (School, Hardware Supply, E-commerce, GM Distributor) enabling better workload planning and resource allocation across the team .
* **New Lead Suggestions at Season Start:** When a segment's buying season begins, the system surfaces leads in that same category who haven't yet been contacted - helping expand coverage precisely when conversion rates are highest.

## FEATURE 07: INTELLIGENCE 

**Customer Health Score** 

* **Early Warning, Not Late Reaction:** When an account's activity starts to decline, it's rarely sudden - there are always early signals. Purchase frequency drops slightly, the gap between orders grows, and engagement with outreach decreases. Without a system to surface these signals early, teams often only realize an account is at risk after revenue has already been lost. Year-over-year movement is already visible per account - a health score surfaces gradual shifts early, when they're easiest to address.
* **Recency & Frequency Scoring:** Each account is scored based on how recently they last ordered and how frequently they typically buy compared against their own historical baseline, not a generic threshold.
* **Trend Deviation Alerts:** When a customer who typically orders every two months goes four months without activity, the system flags it proactively - before the gap becomes a lost account.
* **At-Risk Account List:** A prioritized list of accounts with declining health scores is surfaced in the Daily Command Center - so sales can focus attention where it's most urgently needed this week.

**Customer Health Scale:** At Risk, Needs Attention, Steady, Healthy, Strong.

## FEATURE 08: RISK PREVENTION 

**Stock Awareness Before the Call** 

Imagine this scenario: A sales rep calls a customer, recommends the product they always buy - and only finds out mid-conversation that it's been out of stock for two weeks. The momentum is lost, the customer's confidence is shaken, and a simple check beforehand could have prevented it entirely. Current availability and incoming stock are both trackable giving reps the context they need before recommending any product.

* **Automatic Pre-Call Stock Check:** Before a sales rep contacts a customer, the system automatically checks whether the products that customer typically buys are currently available in the warehouse. This information appears directly in the Pre-Call Brief no manual lookup required.
* **Smart Substitution Suggestions:** If a primary product is out of stock, the system immediately suggests similar or complementary alternatives that are currently available so the sales rep enters the call prepared with a solution, not an apology.
* **Out-of-Stock Duration & Restock Info:** Sales can see how long a product has been unavailable and any estimated restock timeline - enabling honest, informed conversations with customers about delivery expectations and alternative options. Stock gaps are already recorded in the system - surfacing this information in the pre-call brief keeps every conversation prepared.

## FEATURE 09: REVENUE GROWTH 

**Cross-Sell & Upsell Engine** 

Many customers consistently purchase within one product category while accounts with a similar profile purchase across several. This represents untapped revenue that doesn't require finding new customers, only deepening existing relationships. Growth trends by category are already in the data - the engine uses these patterns to identify the right cross-sell conversations per account.

* **Upsell - Within Category:** When a customer regularly buys the entry-level version of a product, the system identifies opportunities to offer the premium or larger variant of the same item. *Example: A customer who consistently purchases standard-size scissors is prompted with the professional or heavy-duty version - a natural next step that often requires just a single conversation.* 
* **Cross-Sell - Complementary Categories:** The engine analyzes what other accounts in the same customer group typically purchase together - and highlights categories that this customer hasn't explored yet. *Example: School-category accounts that buy pencils tend to also purchase erasers and clipboards. If this customer hasn't bought the complementary items, that's a warm conversation starter.* 
* **Gap Analysis:** For every customer, the system shows which products are commonly purchased by similar accounts in their group but haven't yet appeared in their own order history.

## How We're Building This 

Three principles guide every design and build decision for this system.

1. **Power BI Stays as the Analytics Layer:** We are not replacing or rebuilding anything in Power BI. Your dashboards, reports, and data pipelines remain exactly as they are. This recommendation adds an execution layer on top of your existing data source making it actionable for sales reps in the field, without changing what already works.
2. **Sales Reps Don't Need to Be Data Analysts:** The system is designed so that a sales rep never needs to open a data table or write a query. Everything they need - who to call, what to say, what to offer, and what to watch out for is surfaced automatically in plain language, ready to act on.
3. **Data Drives the Action, Not Vice Versa:** Every recommendation, alert, and priority in this system is grounded in your actual purchase history, activity log, and behavioral data. Nothing is guessed or generic it is all derived from patterns that already exist in your data.
